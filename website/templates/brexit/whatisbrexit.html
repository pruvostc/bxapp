What is Brexit?

The UK joined the European Union (EU) in 1973, following a referendum, which at the time showed a majority of 67.2% to join.

Following a recent referendum in 2016, the UK decided to leave the EU. This process is called "brexit" and is a shortcut name for 'Britain' exiting the EU.
